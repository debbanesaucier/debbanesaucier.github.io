Schedule
September 29th, 2023

Paroisse Saint-Pierre-Apôtre

14h00

The wedding ceremony will be held at Paroisse Saint-Pierre-Apôtre at 470 Main St E, Hawkesbury, ON K6A 1A9. Our wedding is semi-formal.

Arrival at Le Château Montebello

You may arrive at any time at Le Château Montebello. A cash bar will be available for guests starting at 18h00. Please note that parking is included for all wedding guests. Guests are welcome to walk around the grounds and inside the hotel lobby.

Reception & Dinner at Le Château Montebello

18h45

A four course dinner will be served at 18h45. Please note that the reception and dinner will be held in an outdoor tent. All main walking paths and the tent area are paved, so heels are welcome and safe to wear.

Staying at Le Château Montebello

Overnight

If you would like to book a room in our hotel block please visit https://book.passkey.com/e/50571151 or call the hotal at 819-423-6341 and mention that you would like to reserve a room under the Debbané and Saucier Wedding. There are a limited number of rooms available until July 29th.

A note on health precautions.

Please note that some in attendance have medical conditions that require additional covid precautions.

We would greatly appreciate that our guests take a covid test within 24 hours of the event and wear a mask while indoors.

Our wedding is a scent-free event. We kindly ask that you refrain from wearing any perfumes, colognes, or other scented products.

Gift registry.

Our gift registries can be found at https://www.myregistry.com/giftlist/alexandreetamy (for items not on Amazon) and https://www.amazon.ca/wedding/alexandre-s-amy-d-ottawa-september-2023/registry/20QG73531DTQ
